Philosophy

Approach

Growing up in Mumbai, a city defined by its diversity, sparked my curiosity about people, their cultures, and the many ways they experience and shape the spaces around them. That curiosity naturally led me to architecture and interior design, where I found a way to combine creativity with a deeper understanding of human experiences. I believe meaningful design begins with empathy — listening, observing, and creating spaces that are thoughtful, functional, and rooted in the people who use them.

Beyond architecture, I draw inspiration from painting, photography, fashion and filmmaking, all of which influence the way I perceive light, materiality, composition and storytelling. Whether I’m developing concepts, detailing interiors or seeing a project through execution, I approach every opportunity with curiosity and a desire to keep learning, creating spaces that are both timeless and deeply connected to the people they serve.”
