Overview

Lead Interior Design Consultants

Complete renovation and execution of an existing high-rise tower — 1B + G + 25 floors + health club + roof — comprising 273 apartments, 296 parking spaces and several retail spaces, with amenities including a swimming pool and gym.

The studio led complete concept design, site execution and handover of all 273 apartments and public areas — covering concept design, 3D renders, specifications, technical drawings, FF&E selection, client coordination, site visits, mock-ups and documentation.

With 25 floors to deliver, the 13th floor was designated as the mock-up floor: twelve apartments were constructed initially, five of which were completed as showcase apartments with full FF&E installations.

The residences embrace a quiet luxury rooted in openness, natural light and refined simplicity — soft stone textures, warm timber, tactile fabrics and muted palettes that hold their calm against the vibrant energy outside.The concept
