Overview

Design & Build

Complete interior design and build for Khaitan & Co. — one of India’s oldest and most recognised full-service law firms.

The design creates a refined and welcoming workplace that reflects the professionalism and credibility of a modern law firm. A palette of warm wood tones, textured neutrals and soft lighting brings balance and sophistication, while clean lines and carefully selected materials create an environment that is both timeless and functional.

  • Converted the project pitch into a live project
  • Worked on interior planning and layouts
  • Assisted with 3ds Max modelling and renders
  • Coordinated material sampling, mock-ups and furniture selections with vendors
  • Prepared design decks for project coordination
  • Attended client meetings, documenting MoMs and design logs
  • Conducted site visits and coordinated with contractors
  • Created conceptual diagrams for technical communication
