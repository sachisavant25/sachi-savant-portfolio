Overview

Lead Interior Design consultants

Concept design and detailing for a villa typology and community mall at Al Khawaneej Avenue — an upcoming residential community by Al Naboodah Group.

The villas pair warm neutrals with landscape views framed by full-height glazing, while the mall composes daylight, timber ceilings and marble surfaces into an open community destination. A comprehensive detailed drawing package was prepared and delivered for the complete villa typology.

  • Unit plans, kitchens, wardrobes and bathrooms
  • Ceiling and flooring details
  • Architectural details and doors
  • Design development on SketchUp
  • Coordination with the visualisation team for final renders
