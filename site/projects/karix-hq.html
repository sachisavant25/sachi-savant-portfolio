Overview

Lead Interior Designers

Complete interior design and build for Karix — a messaging and communication provider — conceived as a dynamic workplace where collaboration forms the heart of the environment.

The planning establishes a strong central social and work core, with focused work strategically distributed around it — letting employees move seamlessly between individual concentration, team collaboration, formal meetings and informal interactions.

  • Supported design development and 3ds Max visualisation
  • Coordinated material sourcing and vendor sampling within budget
  • Managed mock-ups and finish selections for furniture packages
  • Developed coordination decks for FF&E, lighting, graphics and landscape
  • Documented client meetings and maintained design records
  • Led site coordination and contractor communication during execution
  • Produced conceptual diagrams to simplify technical design solutions
