Overview

Lead Interior Design Consultants

Complete concept design for a community retail centre serving Emaar’s The Valley community — the everyday heart of a growing neighbourhood.

Inspired by natural stone, warm timber and soft neutral tones, the design palette creates a timeless and welcoming environment. A harmonious blend of ivory, beige, walnut wood and bronze accents evokes a sense of elegance while maintaining a calm, contemporary atmosphere. The overall composition is designed to maximise light, openness and comfort — a space both sophisticated and inviting for everyday use.

  • Developed the material palette and Pantone colour references
  • Built concept studies in SketchUp for feature walls and public washrooms
  • Prepared material boards and mood imagery for client presentation
  • Coordinated with the visualisation team for final concept renders
