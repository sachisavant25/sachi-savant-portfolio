Overview

Lead Architecture & Interior Design consultants

Complete interior design for six attached townhouses at Al Bada’a — carried from concept design and 3D renders through specifications and technical drawings.

The interiors follow a calm, light-filled palette of pale timber, soft stone and warm white surfaces. A comprehensive detailed drawing package was prepared and delivered for the townhouse typology, alongside full specification schedules coordinated directly with vendors.

  • Concept design and 3D renders
  • Specification packages — sanitaryware, lighting, finishes, FF&E, ironmongery and white goods
  • Vendor coordination and schedule upkeep
  • Unit plans, kitchens, wardrobes and bathrooms
  • Ceiling and flooring details
  • Architectural details and doors
